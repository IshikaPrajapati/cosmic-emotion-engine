# Cosmic Emotion Engine Demo Script

1. Open the web app.
2. Choose either:
   - Start Camera → face-api detects emotion
   - Type your emotion manually
3. App shows detected/typed emotion.
4. App displays cosmic animation themed to the emotion.
5. Click "Play Relaxation Sound" → sound frequency plays.
6. Nova AI generates a short cosmic poetic reflection.
7. Poem appears on screen, complementing the galaxy visuals.
